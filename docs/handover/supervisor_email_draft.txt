Technical Highlights of the AI Solver:
- Training Foundation: We used a learning rate of 0.001 and a batch size of 256 to ensure stable convergence across the curriculum.
- Current Achievement: The model has achieved a 100% optimal solve rate for scrambles up to Depth 4 and approximately 71% at Depth 5.
- Scale: By vectorizing the state encoding process, the system can now handle much higher-capacity training than my previous iterations, allowing the model to master the combinatorial complexity of shorter Optimal Paths with near-perfect accuracy.

Currently, the AI handles shallow optimal solving perfectly (depth 1-4) and serves as an excellent proof-of-concept for how a neural network can learn the group-theoretical structure of the cube.
